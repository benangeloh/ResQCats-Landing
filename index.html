Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free to use?
Yes, ResQCats is completely free to download and use. Our optional premium features and cat essentials delivery service help support our mission.
How do I report a stray cat?
Simply open the app, tap the "Report" button, and follow the prompts to add photos, location, and condition of the cat. Our community will be notified based on proximity.
Do you offer support for cat medical expenses?
While we don't directly fund medical expenses, our partner network of veterinarians offers discounted services. Our community also frequently organizes fundraisers for cats needing extensive care.
Is ResQCats available internationally?
Currently, ResQCats is available in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. We're expanding to more countries soon! Sign up for our newsletter to be notified.
